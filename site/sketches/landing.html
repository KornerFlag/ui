How it works · 01  —  Build-up

It starts at the back.

Every player is tracked from the first touch. The system locks onto the build-up and follows the ball out from defense — no tagging, no manual setup.

How it works · 02  —  Progression

The ball moves. The model follows.

As play progresses, each pass is drawn into a live passing network and possession is measured continuously. You watch the analysis build, pass by pass.

How it works · 03  —  Final third

Every entry becomes a review moment.

When the attack reaches the final third, movement density appears and the moment is flagged as a reviewable clip — ready to drop into a coaching session.
